Condense is proud to partner with some great organisations. We work together to make innovative advances in digital technology, and we win awards for it.

iAward Big Data Innovation of the Year
2018 Tasmania iAwards
Seamap Australia
In partnership with Institute for Marine and Antarctic Studies (IMAS)

For solutions which use large volumes of data (both structured and unstructured) resulting in real strategic analysis and better decisions.

This initiative is a great example of how large volumes of data from a variety of sources and classification practices into a coherant national dataset which is accessible to key decision makers. By allowing users overlay 3rd party data sources other dimensions of structured and unstructured data can be used to produce "big picture" insights.

- Oliver George

iAward Public Sector and Government Markets
2018 Tasmania iAwards
Seamap Australia
In partnership with Institute for Marine and Antarctic Studies (IMAS)

Digital projects that deliver the digitisation of citizen services and improve efficiencies in the machinery of Government.

Seamap Australia shows the value that can be unlocked by providing access to the treasuretrove of data captured by scietists and researchers. Working with large organisations in the public sector requires an understanding of the sometimes- complex environment and the difficulties of manipulating different sources for large amounts of data to achieve meaningful results. This close partnership has yielded meaningful results. Oliver George
